Phronesis: the idea behind Phrona

Phrona is named after phronesis — a Greek concept best translated as the ability to apply wisdom in practice. It refers to the capacity to make the right choice in complex situations, not only based on knowledge, but also on insight into context.

Health is rarely black and white. What works for one person does not work for another. Nutrition, movement, sleep, stress, culture and home situation all continuously influence each other. We believe that good guidance starts with understanding that context. Not blindly applying a guideline, but asking the right why question. Because knowledge without context is only half as powerful — advice only makes a real difference once it is clear what is actually going on.

The evolutionary and behavioural side also plays a role here. Many of our habits around eating and movement are deeply rooted in biology, upbringing and social environment. We do not ignore that — we work with it.

In practice, this means we do not start with a standard plan, but with a conversation. What is your situation, what have you already tried and what is happening in the background? From there, we build something together that fits you.

The result is guidance that suits you as a person, not an average. And that is exactly what we try to bring into every consultation, taking the time and attention to arrive at something that truly works.
