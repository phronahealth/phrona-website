Programmes

Corporate support is always tailored, but broadly consists of three pillars: analysis of the workplace, working with teams and targeted guidance for individual employees.

Workplace assessment

On-site observation, analysis of posture, workload and environmental factors.

  • Short intake with HR/management
  • Observation and conversations with employees
  • Practical quick wins directly at the workplace

Workshops & team programmes

Group sessions on topics such as vitality, stress, physical activity and nutrition at work.

  • Interactive sessions with concrete assignments
  • Focus on applying insights in daily practice
  • Optional follow-up sessions with progress monitoring

Individual guidance

Personal support for employees who want to improve resilience, energy and sustainable work enjoyment.

  • Focus on behaviour, routines and physical load in work & sports
  • Concrete actions that lead to noticeable improvement straight away
  • Regular progress meetings with clear, measurable goals
  • Feedback to the organisation only at process level (never content-related or medical)

Approach

We work in clear steps, with fixed feedback moments. For longer programmes, progress and results are recorded in reports, so that choices are transparent and well-founded.

1. Introduction & clarifying goals

Meeting with HR or management to discuss the question, organisational context and desired outcomes (for example, less absenteeism, higher employability or better collaboration).

2. Analysis

Interviews, on-site observations and analysis of tasks, workload and work processes. Where relevant, we include existing data such as absenteeism figures or employee satisfaction.

3. Plan & proposal

Concrete proposal with interventions (workshops, coaching, workplace assessments), planning and measurable indicators. This is documented in a concise report that forms the basis for approval.

4. Implementation

Delivery of sessions, coaching and workplace visits. Employees receive practical tools and assignments that can be applied directly in their daily work.

5. Progress & feedback

Interim feedback to the client organisation with observations, participation, bottlenecks and first results. For longer programmes this is done periodically in the form of brief progress reports.

6. Evaluation & final report

Joint evaluation of objectives and achieved results. You receive a final report with a summary of the programme, key insights and concrete recommendations for embedding and possible follow-up.

For programmes spanning several months, we record developments in reports, so that you have insight into input, progress and effects within the organisation at each stage.

Workplace assessment

A workplace assessment focuses on the concrete situation of employees: working posture, workstation setup, task structure and recovery moments. We look at what can be adjusted immediately and what requires structural attention.

  • Observation of workplace and tasks
  • Short conversations with involved employees
  • Directly applicable tips and adjustments

Afterwards you receive a concise report with:

  • Overview of key findings
  • Short-term advice (quick wins)
  • Recommendations for long-term policy, setup or training

Proposal & planning

Every programme is tailored. Based on your request and the desired scope, we prepare a proposal with a clear overview of hours, travel costs and any reporting. For longer programmes, we explicitly include the moments of progress and final reporting in the planning.
