Our philosophy

Read more →

Health is rarely black and white. What works depends on your context — from nutrition and movement to stress, environment and habits. At Phrona, we therefore look beyond isolated advice: we aim to understand what is really going on, so guidance truly fits.

Our name comes from phronesis, meaning practical wisdom. Not just knowing what is right, but understanding what the right choice is in your situation — and building from there together.

Do I need a referral?

Physiotherapy often does not require a referral; dietetics usually doesn’t either. Reimbursement depends on your policy.

How soon can I be seen?

Usually within 3 working days.

How do I request urgent care?
