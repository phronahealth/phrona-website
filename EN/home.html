Our philosophy

Read more →

Health requires more than knowledge alone. At Phrona we combine science, practical experience and attention for the person behind the complaint. We look at behaviour, lifestyle, culture and motivation to offer support that truly fits the life you lead.

Our name comes from phronesis : practical wisdom. The ability to make healthy, clear choices in complex situations. That is the foundation of how we work.

Do I need a referral?

Physiotherapy often does not require a referral; dietetics usually doesn’t either. Reimbursement depends on your policy.

How soon can I be seen?

Usually within 3 working days. For urgent issues, please call the practice.

Do you offer online consultations?

Yes, where appropriate we can schedule online consultations. You can book via the website.
