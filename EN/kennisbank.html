Frequently asked questions

Do I need a referral?

Physiotherapy: often not required, reimbursement depends on your policy. Dietetics: 3 hours basic cover per year.

How long does a treatment session take?

Physio ±30 minutes. Dietetics 30–60 minutes.

Do you offer online consultations?

Yes, when appropriate.

Are treatments reimbursed?

Depends on your policy.

How soon can I be seen?

Usually within a few days.

What should I bring?

ID, insurance details, medical info.

Do you provide home visits?

Yes, when medically necessary.

Can I cancel my appointment?

Up to 24 hours free of charge.

Do you support chronic conditions?

Yes, including long-term pain and COPD.

Weight loss or gain support?

Yes, tailored dietetics guidance.

Sports rehabilitation?

Yes, including return-to-sport.

Pregnancy-related complaints?

Yes, both physiotherapy and dietetics.
