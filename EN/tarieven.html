Reimbursement & insurance

Most treatments are (partly) reimbursed through your health insurance. Below you find the general rules per service. Always check your own policy to see exactly what applies.

Physiotherapy

Often reimbursed from supplementary insurance. Basic cover applies for certain indications or referrals.

Dietetics

3 hours per year reimbursed from basic insurance. Additional hours via supplementary cover. No referral needed.

Corporate support

Usually paid by the employer. Proposal and invoicing are arranged directly with the company.

Important: exact reimbursement amounts and coverage differ per insurer and policy. Always check your policy terms.
