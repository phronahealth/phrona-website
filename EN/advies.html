How does it work?

Advisory always starts with listening carefully. Only when the question is clear does an answer have value.

1. Analysis

We map the situation: what is happening, what has already been tried, and what is the concrete goal? This prevents superficial advice.

2. Advisory plan

Based on the analysis, a concrete recommendation or plan follows. This may be a conversation, a document or a set of recommendations — depending on the question and its scope.

3. Guidance & implementation

Where desired, I support the implementation of the advice. This ensures it does not remain a good intention but is actually translated into action.

Advisory engagements are always tailored. The scope, format and duration are aligned with the specific question — from a single conversation to a longer support programme.
